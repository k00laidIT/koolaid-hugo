I have been fortunate to be selected as a delegate for [Cloud Field Day 12](https://techfieldday.com/event/cfd12/) next week, November 3-5, 2021. This will be the first Tech Field Day event being done with in person attendees since the beginning of the pandemic and I'm happy to say I am on my way to San Jose, CA to be in attendance. While I am definitely excited about being able to travel about this I am most excited about the exciting slate of vendors that will be presenting on all things Cloudy. Thus far the delegate panel appears to be very well attended and includes a number of friends and acquaintances such as [Nico Stein](https://twitter.com/NicoAStein) and [Nathan Bennett](https://twitter.com/vNathanBennett).

## Disclosure

In the interests of full disclosure it is worth noting that [Gesalt IT](https://gestaltit.com/), the company behind [Tech Field Day](https://www.techfieldday.com), is covering the costs of my expenses for this trip up to and including travel, lodging and meals while on site. That said I have had no stipulations or requests on commentary from either Gesalt IT or presenting sponsors. In the week prior to the event I was invited to be a guest on the On-Premise IT podcast discussion the premise "[The Cloud Is Finally Ready for the Enterprise."](https://gestaltit.com/podcast/zach/the-cloud-is-finally-ready-for-the-enterprise/)
