Ahh, that time of year when geeks pull that long forgotten blog site out of the closet, dust it of and make promises of love and content: [\#vDM30in30](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23vdm30in30). If you aren't familiar with the idea, vDM30in30 is short for [Virtual Design Master 30 blog posts in 30 days](http://discoposse.com/2016/10/20/vdm30in30-november-2016-its-on/), an idea championed by [Eric Wright](https://twitter.com/discoposse) of [discoposse ](http://discoposse.com/)fame to get bloggers out there to work their way through regular generation of content. As you can see from this site new content is pretty rare so something like this is a welcome excuse to focus and get some stuff out there. vDM30in30 runs through the month of November and the best way to follow along with the content is to track the hashtag on twitter. **So What's the Plan?** I'm a planner by nature so if I don't at least have a general idea this isn't going to work at all. The good news is I've got quite a few posts that I've been meaning to work on for some time so I'm going to be cleaning out my closet this week and get those out there. So the full schedule is going to look like this:

- Week of Nov 1: random posts I've never quite finished but need to be released
- Week of Nov 7: focus on all the new hotness coming from Veeam Software
- Week of Nov 14: VMware's upcoming vSphere 6.5 release
- Week of Nov 21: randomness about community, career and navel gazing in general
 
 I'm really looking forward to participating this year as I do believe that a lot of growth comes from successfully forming out thoughts and putting them down. Hope you find some of this hopeful, if there is anything you'd like to see in the space feel free to comment.
