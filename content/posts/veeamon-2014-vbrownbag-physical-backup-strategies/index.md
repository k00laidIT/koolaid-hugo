What aided to my discomfort was that the whole purpose of this was to show a method I was using to backup physical servers in a Veeam world, something there was no answer to at the time. That morning it leaked that Veeam was announcing what is now known as Veeam Agent for Windows and my friend [Rick Vanover](<Https://twitter.com/Rick Vanover>) was following me on the same stage to announce it. Talk about your topic becoming irrelevant in record time...
